What is EventGo?

EventGo is a way for those who may be too far or too "under the weather" to attend special events of those special people in their life. Never miss an event again!

What can I use EventGo for? What is an event?

An event is any big social gathering. That can be a weddings, birthdays, funerals, or anything else you might need it for!

What does EventGo provide?

EventGo gives hosts the ability to stream their event for their online guests, as well as allows for loved ones to send the hosts hopeful, and loving messages or take pictures, either of the event or the guests, that can be saved later by the host, after the event. Guests are also able to save the pictures that they take during the event.

How does EventGo work?

When the hosts creates the "event", they create a personal link to invite loved ones. After the event is finished, the hosts are given the opportunity to save messages and photos sent by the guests. After the event has ended and the mementos are saved by the hosts and guests,the system clears everything. Make sure you save everything before finishing!
